# Pharmacy Sales & Performance Analysis
## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data from a European pharmacy chain distributor operating across multiple countries. The goal is to understand how revenue and profitability vary across pharmacy networks, product categories, and geographic regions, and to identify the key factors driving business performance.
The analysis combines Python-based exploratory data analysis (EDA) with an interactive Power BI dashboard to uncover trends, compare performance across pharmacy sizes, and derive actionable business insights.

## Business Problem
Pharmacy distributors operate across different markets with varying demand patterns, product mixes, and pharmacy sizes. Identifying which products, locations, and pharmacy segments generate the highest revenue and profitability can help businesses optimize their strategy and improve overall performance.
## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas,Matplotlib,Seaborn,Plotly) - Data cleaning and exploratory data analysis
- Power BI - Interactive dashboard development
- Excel - Initial data inspection and preperation
## Analysis Workflow
- Cleaned and prepared the dataset by checking for inconsistencies and missing values.
- Conducted exploratory data analysis using Python to understand data distributions and identify key patterns.
- Analyzed revenue and margin performance across product categories, pharmacy sizes, and geographic regions.
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize KPIs, trends, and comparative performance metrics.
- Derived insights and business recommendations based on analytical findings.
## Dashboard Overview
### Business Overview
This page highlights key business metrics including revenue growth, margin percentage, and revenue distribution across pharmacy sizes, countries, and locations.
### Product Performance Analysis
This section evaluates product category performance, identifies top products by revenue and margin, and compares branded vs generic product sales.
### Pharmacy Network Analysis
This analysis focuses on the pharmacy network structure, comparing revenue performance across pharmacy sizes and identifying top performing pharmacies.
## Key Insights
- The company maintains an overall margin of approximately 28% with moderate revenue growth.
- Medium-sized pharmacies contribute the highest total revenue due to their larger presence in the network.
- Large pharmacies generate higher average revenue per store, indicating stronger individual performance.
- Branded and prescription products contribute the majority of total revenue.
- Urban and suburhan markets generate significantly higher revenue compared to rural areas.
## Business Recommendations
- Focus on expanding sales of high-performing ang high-margin products.
- Improve profitability of products with high revenue but lower margins.
- Strengthen distribution strategies in high-performing urban and suburhan regions.
- Monitor product demand trends to optimize inventory planning and product mix.
